    Our Story

    Pure, Potent and Packed with Tradition!

    Shree Vitthalkrupa International Established in 2015, Shree Vitthalkrupa International is a leading import-export enterprise specializing in high-quality agricultural commodities. Under the leadership of Chairman Subhash Punjaji Ranjwe and Chief Mananging Director Nikhil Subhash Ranjwe, the company has cultivated a strong presence in international markets, including Sri Lanka, Oman, Dubai, and Bangladesh.

    Core Offerings The firm is dedicated to sourcing and delivering premium agricultural products, including:
    Spices: Turmeric fingers, raw turmeric, dry chilies , ginger cardamom.
    Vegetables and Fruits: Onions, lemons, and garlic.

    By maintaining rigorous quality standards, efficient logistics, and customer-centric services, Shree Vitthalkrupa International continues to be a trusted partner in the global trade industry.
